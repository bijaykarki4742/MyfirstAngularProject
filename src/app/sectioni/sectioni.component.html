Taylor's Verify (excluding external award programmes)

In our bid to counter the burgeoning use of fraudulent academic credentials and counterfeit degrees, Taylor’s provides an online service called Taylor’s Verify to allow potential employers and/or sponsors to instantly verify the authenticity of the parchments of homegrown programmes conferred to its graduates. For more information on this service, please logon to https:// verify.taylors.edu.my.
